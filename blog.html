Circus Freak is a Compliment

I wouldn't have imagined that circus training would completely change how I view myself and others, but it has been the biggest blessing in my life.

Some of the best benefits of circus training have been:

  • Sense of self worth
  • Better self esteem
  • Stronger body
  • Healthier lifestyle

One of the first things you develop as a circus artist is your grip strength, both in vertical and horizontal orientations. This can be done on silks (fabric, tissu) and trapeze.
